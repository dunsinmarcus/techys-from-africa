Techys From Africa

We are a community-focused project that seeks to bring African techies, all over the world, together in one unity, to form a strong symbol - a powerful force.

About

The inspiration for the project came after witnessing the recent rise in the (previously little) amount of Africans interested in tech, and realizing the vast array of currently untappped but tappable potentials if many Africans had a more progressive and technological orientation.

Using NFTs as a medium for proliferating the meme and recruiting others who can see the dream, the community is bound to grow into a full-fledged movement, after first gaining a wide mental establishment.

We aim to achieve this by first forming a community with the few who already have this oriental exposure, and then reaching those who don't, later on, as the community grows wider.
